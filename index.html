What is AndyRat Beta Program?

AndyRat Beta Program is a program designated for people who would like to experience new features before everyone else and provide us feedbacks. We love to hear your feedback, so join our beta program today!

After applying, you will get an email from us within 48 hours for detailed instruction on how to start your beta testing along with how to provide feedbacks to us. This application is 100% free to apply, and our beta testing program will not charge you during the testing process nor will we ask for sensitive information from you.
